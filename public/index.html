About

Brit in Texas. Scientist come developer.

Last year I took advantage of my US passport to move to Austin and become a web developer.

My domain is everything front end - JavaScript, React, HTML5 & CSS3 are my specialties. And I'm a real sucker for clean, calm design (as I hope is evident in my work).

Having said that, I'm handy with back end technologies too. Give me a challenge in any part of the MERN stack and I'm happy to get stuck in.

Before working in tech I studied biomedicine at university, conducted laser bioeffect research and worked in advertising in London.
